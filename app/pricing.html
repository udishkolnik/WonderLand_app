â“ Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I cancel my membership?

You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. You'll retain access to the platform until the end of your billing period, and any ventures you're involved in will continue with your existing equity stake.

How is equity distributed in ventures?

AliceSolutionsGroup typically keeps 15-30% equity in ventures, with the remaining equity distributed among venture participants based on their contribution, role, and agreement terms.

What if a venture fails?

Our portfolio approach means that even if some ventures fail, successful ones create significant returns. We learn from failures and apply those lessons to future ventures.

Can I join multiple ventures?

Yes! Members can participate in multiple ventures simultaneously, building a diversified portfolio of equity stakes and learning opportunities.

What support do I get as a member?

You get access to our full platform, legal templates, tech scaffolding, analytics tools, and the support of our community. We provide guidance but don't make promises about venture success.
