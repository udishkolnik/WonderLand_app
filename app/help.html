Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite team members?

Go to the Team page and click "Invite Member". Enter their email address and they'll receive an invitation to join your team.

How do I create a new venture?

Click the "New Venture" button on your dashboard or go to "My Ventures" and click "Create Venture". Fill in the details and start building!

How do I change my profile settings?

Click on your profile in the header dropdown and select "My Profile" or go to Settings to customize your preferences.

How do I get notifications?

Go to Settings > Notifications to configure your notification preferences. You can choose email, push notifications, or both.
