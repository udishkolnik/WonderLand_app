Sarah's Story

Sarah was scrolling through LinkedIn when she came across a post from Udi Shkolnik about SmartStart. The post described a micro-venture studio that helps people turn practical product ideas into profitable SaaS businesses.

Intrigued, Sarah clicked through to the SmartStart website. She was immediately drawn to the clean, professional design and the clear value proposition. The idea of having structured support, legal templates, and a collaborative community resonated with her.

Key Insight: Sarah realized that SmartStart wasn't just another incubator - it was a complete ecosystem designed to reduce the friction of venture creation.

After reading through the pricing page and understanding the CAD $100/month membership fee, Sarah decided to take the plunge. She was particularly excited about the equity participation model and the portfolio approach to risk management.

Sarah created her account and was immediately impressed by the onboarding process. The platform guided her through setting up her profile, including her skills, experience, and areas of interest.
