Sarah's Sprint 0 Journey

With her problem statement approved, Sarah moved into Sprint 0 - the critical phase where she would scope her MVP and build her initial team. This phase was designed to be completed in less than a week, forcing clarity and focus.

Sarah used SmartStart's MVP scoping template to define her minimum viable product. She focused on the core features that would validate her hypothesis: project management basics, team mood tracking, and simple wellness check-ins.

Key Insight: Sarah realized that her MVP didn't need to be feature-complete - it just needed to solve the core problem and validate the market need.

Building her team was the next crucial step. Sarah used SmartStart's team matching algorithm to find collaborators with complementary skills. She was looking for a developer, a designer, and a marketing specialist.
