Sarah's Beta Testing Journey

With her MVP complete, Sarah was ready to test it with real users. This was a critical phase where she would validate her assumptions and gather feedback to improve the product.

Sarah used SmartStart's beta testing tools to recruit test users from the community and beyond. She created a structured testing plan that included user interviews, usability testing, and feedback collection.

Key Insight: Sarah discovered that her users loved the mood tracking feature, but found the project management tools too complex for their needs.
