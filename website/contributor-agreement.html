1. Agreement Overview

This Contributor Agreement governs the relationship between contributors and AliceSolutionsGroup regarding intellectual property, equity participation, and revenue sharing in ventures created through the SmartStart platform.

2. Intellectual Property Assignment

All intellectual property created by contributors in connection with ventures through the SmartStart platform is assigned to the venture entity, subject to the equity and revenue sharing terms outlined in this agreement.

3. Equity Participation

Contributors may receive equity in ventures based on their contribution level, skills, and time commitment. Equity percentages are determined on a case-by-case basis and documented in individual venture agreements.

4. Revenue Sharing

AliceSolutionsGroup may retain 15-30% equity in ventures created through the platform, with the remaining equity distributed among contributors based on their contributions and agreements.

5. Contributor Responsibilities

Contributors are responsible for maintaining confidentiality, meeting agreed-upon deliverables, and complying with all applicable laws and regulations in their work.

6. Confidentiality

All contributors must maintain strict confidentiality regarding venture information, business strategies, and proprietary technology developed through the platform.

7. Work Product

All work product created in connection with ventures becomes the property of the venture entity, subject to the equity and revenue sharing terms of this agreement.

8. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days notice. Upon termination, existing equity and revenue sharing arrangements remain in effect for completed work.

9. Dispute Resolution

Disputes arising under this agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the laws of Canada, Province of Ontario.

10. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Canada, with jurisdiction in the Province of Ontario.

11. Contact Information

For questions about this agreement, please contact us at info@alicesolutionsgroup.com
