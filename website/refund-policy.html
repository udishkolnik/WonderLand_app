Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025
Effective Date: January 2025

1. Refund Eligibility

Membership fees are generally non-refundable. However, we may provide refunds in certain circumstances as outlined in this policy.

2. 30-Day Refund Window

New members may request a full refund within 30 days of their initial membership payment, provided they have not actively participated in any ventures or used premium platform features.

3. Refund Process

Refund requests must be submitted in writing to support@alicesolutionsgroup.com within the applicable time frame. All requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

4. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days and will be credited to the original payment method used for the membership fee.

5. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be considered for members who have used limited platform features but have not actively participated in ventures or used premium services.

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds: venture participation fees, premium service charges, and any fees associated with completed transactions or services.

7. Cancellation

Members may cancel their membership at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing period, and no refund will be provided for the unused portion of the current period.

8. Dispute Resolution

Members who disagree with a refund decision may request a review by contacting info@alicesolutionsgroup.com. All disputes will be resolved in accordance with our Terms of Service.

9. Policy Changes

This refund policy may be updated from time to time. Members will be notified of significant changes via email with 30 days notice.

10. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact us at support@alicesolutionsgroup.com
