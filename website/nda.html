Last Updated: January 2025
Effective Date: January 2025

1. Purpose

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) protects confidential information shared between AliceSolutionsGroup and its members, contributors, and partners in connection with venture development and platform operations.

2. Definition of Confidential Information

Confidential information includes, but is not limited to, business plans, financial information, technical specifications, customer lists, marketing strategies, and any other proprietary information disclosed in connection with ventures or platform operations.

3. Obligations of Receiving Party

The receiving party agrees to maintain strict confidentiality, use the information solely for the purpose of venture development, and not disclose it to third parties without written consent.

4. Exceptions

This agreement does not apply to information that is publicly available, independently developed, or required to be disclosed by law or court order.

5. Duration

Confidentiality obligations continue for a period of five (5) years from the date of disclosure, regardless of the termination of any other agreements.

6. Return of Information

Upon termination of the relationship, all confidential information must be returned or destroyed, with written confirmation of destruction provided.

7. Remedies

Breach of this agreement may result in injunctive relief, monetary damages, and other legal remedies available under applicable law.

8. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Canada, Province of Ontario.

9. Contact Information

For questions about this NDA, please contact us at info@alicesolutionsgroup.com
