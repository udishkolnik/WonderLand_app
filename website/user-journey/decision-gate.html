Sarah's Decision Gate Journey

After 30 days of intensive work, Sarah reached the decision gate - the critical moment where she would decide whether to scale, pause, or sunset her venture based on the data and feedback she had collected.

Sarah used SmartStart's decision gate reporting tools to analyze her venture's performance across all key metrics: user feedback, market validation, technical feasibility, and team dynamics.
