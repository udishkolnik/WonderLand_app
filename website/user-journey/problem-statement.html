Sarah's Problem Discovery

Sarah spent her first few days on SmartStart exploring the platform and connecting with other members. She was particularly interested in the problem statement template, which guided her through defining her venture's core problem.

Drawing from her experience managing remote creative teams, Sarah identified a significant pain point: traditional project management tools don't account for the unique needs of creative professionals, especially when it comes to team wellness and mood tracking.

Sarah's Problem Statement

Problem: Remote creative teams struggle with project management tools that don't account for creative workflows, team wellness, or mood tracking, leading to decreased productivity and team burnout.

Target Market: Creative agencies, design studios, and remote creative teams (5-50 people)

Market Size: $2.3B creative services market with 40% remote work adoption

Sarah used SmartStart's market validation tools to research her target market. She discovered that 73% of creative professionals report feeling disconnected from their teams when working remotely, and 68% struggle with maintaining creative momentum in virtual environments.

Key Insight: Sarah realized that her problem wasn't just about project management - it was about creating a holistic environment that supports both productivity and creative well-being.

With her problem statement clearly defined, Sarah was ready to move to the next phase: Sprint 0, where she would scope her MVP and build her initial team.
